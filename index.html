History of Bricks

Bricks have been used for construction for thousands of years. They are one of the oldest building materials known to man. The earliest bricks were made from mud and dried in the sun.

Types of Bricks

There are many different types of bricks, including clay bricks, concrete bricks, and fly ash bricks. Each type has its own unique properties and uses.

Uses of Bricks

Bricks are primarily used for building walls, pavements, and other elements in masonry construction. They have been used in many iconic structures around the world.
